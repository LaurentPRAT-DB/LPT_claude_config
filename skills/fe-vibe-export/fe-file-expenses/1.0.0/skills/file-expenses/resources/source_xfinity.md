# Xfinity Internet Receipt Download

## Overview
Download Xfinity/Comcast internet bill PDFs for expense reporting.

## Authentication
- URL: https://login.xfinity.com/
- Login: Email/Username + password
- May require 2FA

## Navigation Steps

### 1. Login to Xfinity
```
https://login.xfinity.com/
```

### 2. Access Account & Billing
After login, navigate to billing:
```
https://customer.xfinity.com/#/billing
```

Or click **"Billing"** from account menu.

### 3. View Bill History
The billing page shows:
- Current balance
- Auto Pay status
- Recent statements

### 4. Download Bill PDF
1. Click on a statement date to view details
2. Look for **"Download PDF"** or **"View Statement"** option
3. Download the PDF

### 5. Access Historical Bills
1. Look for **"View past statements"** or billing history
2. Select the month you need
3. Download PDF

## Bill PDF Contents
Xfinity bill PDFs include:
- Account number
- Service address
- Billing period
- Internet service charges
- Equipment fees (if applicable)
- Taxes and fees
- Total amount

## Expense Category
**Internet / WiFi (Home)** - Use this category in Emburse

## Expense Allocation
If internet is for home office:
- May allocate percentage for business use
- Document allocation method
- Typical business allocation: 25-50%

## Email Upload to Emburse
After downloading, email receipts to: `receipt@ca1.chromeriver.com`
- Subject: Amount (e.g., "50.00 USD")
- Attach the PDF bill

## Tips
- Statements available for ~24 months online
- Can also access via Xfinity My Account app
- Paper statements may be available by mail if enabled
