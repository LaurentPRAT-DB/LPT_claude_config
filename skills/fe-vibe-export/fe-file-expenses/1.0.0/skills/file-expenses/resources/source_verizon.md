# Verizon Mobile Receipt Download

## Overview
Download Verizon Wireless bill PDFs for expense reporting. Monthly cell phone bills can be downloaded as PDFs.

## Authentication
- URL: https://www.verizon.com/signin
- Login: Phone number + password
- May require 2FA via SMS

## Account Info
- Account shows all lines on plan
- Bills are monthly (billing cycle varies by account)
- Auto Pay status visible on dashboard

## Navigation Steps

### 1. Login to My Verizon
```
https://www.verizon.com/signin
```

Enter phone number, then password on next screen.

### 2. Access Bill Overview
After login, navigate to Bill Overview:
```
https://www.verizon.com/digital/nsa/secure/ui/ngd/bill/overview
```

Or click **"Bill overview"** from the dashboard shortcuts.

### 3. View Bill History
The Bill Overview page shows:
- Current balance
- Auto Pay status
- Bill history chart (last 6 months)
- Payment history

### 4. Access Bill Details
Click **"Bill details"** to see the current bill breakdown:
```
https://www.verizon.com/digital/nsa/secure/ui/ngd/bill/billdetails
```

Bill details show:
- Plans cost
- Products & plan perks
- Surcharges
- Taxes & gov fees
- Total

### 5. Download Bill PDF
From the Bill Details page:
1. Click **"Review Bill PDF"** link at top of page
2. PDF opens in viewer or downloads

Alternative:
- Click **"Documents & receipts"** in Related Information section
- Select bill period and download

### 6. Access Historical Bills
To get previous months' bills:
1. Go to Bill Overview
2. Click **"Documents & receipts"** in the Related Information section
3. This shows all available bills and receipts
4. Select the month you need
5. Download the PDF

Alternative method:
1. Click **"Review history"** button in Bill History section
2. Select the month you need
3. Navigate to that bill's details and download PDF

## Bill PDF Contents
Verizon bill PDFs include:
- Account number
- Billing period
- Itemized charges by line
- Plan details
- Surcharges and taxes breakdown
- Payment information
- Total amount due

## Expense Category
**Mobile/Cellphone MobSec** - Use this category in Emburse

## Expense Allocation
If billing covers personal + business use:
- Consider 50% allocation for business
- Or calculate based on business call/data percentage
- Document allocation method for audit purposes

## Email Upload to Emburse
After downloading, email receipts to: `receipt@ca1.chromeriver.com`
- Subject: Amount (e.g., "80.00 USD" for business portion)
- Attach the PDF bill
- Add note about allocation if partial

## Tips
- Bills available for ~18 months
- Download all relevant months at once to save time
- Note: Amount shown is total bill, may need to allocate business portion
